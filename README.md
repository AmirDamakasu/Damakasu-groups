## About DAMAKASU MULTI-LINK INVESTIMENT LTD

DAMAKASU MULTI-LINK INVESTIMENT LTD is a professional industrial raw materials and manufacturing supply company. We specialize in sourcing, procurement, storage, and distribution of high-quality industrial and agricultural raw materials for manufacturers, food processors, FMCG companies, and pharmaceutical producers worldwide. Through strategic supplier partnerships, strict quality control, and efficient logistics management, we help businesses maintain stable production and reduce supply-chain risks.

### Key Features & Services

* **Bulk Raw Material Supply:** Large-volume supply contracts for manufacturers.
* **Strategic Sourcing:** Reliable supplier identification and procurement management.
* **Quality Inspection:** Rigorous testing, grading, and compliance verification.
* **Warehousing & Inventory Management:** Secure climate-controlled storage and stock management.
* **Logistics & Distribution:** Domestic and international delivery solutions with real-time tracking.
* **Procurement Consulting:** Supply-chain optimization and sourcing strategy support.

### Industrial Commodities & Products

* **Sesame Seeds:** Premium export-quality seeds for food manufacturing and oil extraction.
* **Cashew Nuts:** High-grade raw cashew nuts for food processing industries.
* **Soybeans:** Protein-rich soybeans for animal feed mills and industrial processing.
* **Industrial Grade Rice:** Bulk rice supply for food processing and manufacturing.
* **Yellow Maize:** High-quality maize for feed mills and food production.
* **Hibiscus Flower:** Premium dried hibiscus calyces for beverage and pharmaceutical processors.
* **Coffee Beans:** High-grade green and roasted coffee beans for roasters and processors.
* **Ginger:** Split dried and fresh ginger roots for beverage and extraction industries.
* **Cocoa Beans:** High-quality fermented cocoa beans for chocolate and food production.

To support manufacturers with reliable access to quality raw materials through efficient sourcing, strategic partnerships, and world-class supply-chain solutions.
